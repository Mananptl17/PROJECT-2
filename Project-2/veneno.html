Interior

The Lamborghini Veneno is, without a doubt, one of the most radical, high-performance sports cars to ever hit the automotive scene. It’s angular, high-tech design turned heads when it was first introduced at the 2013 Geneva Motor Show. The limited-production car series was developed to celebrate Lamborghini’s 50th anniversary and what a gift it was to collectors. Only nine roadsters and five coupes were produced by the Italian automobile manufacturer between 2013 and 2015.

Exterior

One of the most expensive production cars to ever be released, the Veneno was enjoyed by more than most, in a virtual way, by fans of the Asphalt racing games with the car featuring in versions 8 & 9 as a playable gamer favorite.Shaped to emphasize the feeling of acceleration from nose to tail, the Veneno every crease, fold, and vent serves a purpose. From drawing air into the engine, to cooling the brakes or aiding aerodynamic air flow, nothing is there just for show.
